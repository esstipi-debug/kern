## 🚀 Getting Started (5 Minutes)

### **Step 1: Download**
- Click **Releases** → Download latest version
- Or clone: `git clone https://github.com/yourusername/supply-chain-optimization.git`

### **Step 2: Prepare Your Data**
Format required:
```
Date          | Product | Quantity | Lead_Time | Unit_Cost
2024-01-01   | SKU-A   | 100      | 7         | 50
2024-01-02   | SKU-A   | 105      | 7         | 50
2024-01-03   | SKU-B   | 250      | 14        | 25
```

### **Step 3: Load & Calculate**
- Open template file
- Paste your data
- Formulas calculate automatically
- View recommendations immediately

### **Step 4: Act**
- Follow "Recommendations" sheet
- Update supplier orders
- Monitor alerts
- Track results
